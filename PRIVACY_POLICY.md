# Privacy Policy for Daily Questions

**Last updated: December 18, 2024**

## Introduction

Daily Questions ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Daily Question Answers**: Your text and audio responses to daily questions
- **Voice Recordings**: Audio files when you choose to record answers (stored locally only)
- **User Preferences**: Theme selections, notification settings, and other app preferences

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: iOS version, device model (for app functionality only)
- **Subscription Status**: Information about your premium subscription status through Apple's StoreKit

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide daily reflection questions
- Store your answers locally on your device
- Display insights and analytics based on your answers
- Manage your premium subscription
- Send optional daily reminder notifications

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- All your answers (text and audio) are stored **locally on your device only**
- We do NOT upload, sync, or transmit your answers to any external servers
- Your reflection data never leaves your device
- If you delete the app, all your data is permanently deleted

### iCloud Storage
- Your data is NOT stored in iCloud
- Your subscription status is managed through Apple's secure systems

## Permissions We Request

### Microphone Access
- Required for: Recording audio answers to daily questions
- Used only when: You choose to record an audio answer
- Can be disabled: Yes, you can answer with text only

### Speech Recognition
- Required for: Transcribing audio answers (optional feature)
- Used only when: You enable voice-to-text
- Can be disabled: Yes, this is an optional feature

### Notifications
- Required for: Daily reminder notifications
- Used only when: You enable reminders in settings
- Can be disabled: Yes, notifications are completely optional

## Third-Party Services

### Apple App Store & StoreKit
- We use Apple's StoreKit for subscription management
- Apple processes subscription payments according to their privacy policy
- We receive only subscription status information (active/inactive)

### No Analytics or Tracking
- We do NOT use analytics services
- We do NOT track your behavior
- We do NOT share data with third parties
- We do NOT use advertising services

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your data (it's stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (uninstall the app)
- Disable microphone, speech recognition, or notifications at any time
- Cancel your subscription at any time

## Children's Privacy

Daily Questions is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect server-side data, there is minimal privacy risk.

## Subscription Information

- Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store
- Payment is charged to your Apple ID account
- Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled 24 hours before the period ends
- Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings
- No refunds for unused portions of subscription periods

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
- Posting a notification in the app (for significant changes)

## Data Retention

- Data is retained on your device until you delete the app
- Deleting the app permanently deletes all local data
- Subscription records are maintained by Apple per their policies

## International Users

Daily Questions is available worldwide. All data processing occurs locally on your device. Subscription processing is handled by Apple according to their international policies.

## Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we don't collect or share personal data (all data is local), most CCPA obligations don't apply. However:
- You have the right to know what data we collect (see above)
- You have the right to delete your data (uninstall the app)
- We do NOT sell your personal information

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union:
- **Legal basis for processing**: Your consent and contractual necessity
- **Data controller**: Achilleas Tzenetopoulos
- **Data transfers**: No data is transferred outside your device
- **Right to access**: You can access all your data on your device
- **Right to erasure**: Delete the app to erase all data
- **Right to portability**: Data is stored in standard formats on your device
- **Right to object**: You can disable any permission at any time
