Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 28, 2025

Introduction

Daily Question ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Information We Don't Collect

Daily Question is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or answers to external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Local Storage

All of your answers to daily questions are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you manually choose to back it up through your device's backup system (such as iCloud).

Notifications

Daily Question uses local notifications to remind you to answer your daily question. These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve any external servers or data transmission.

Children's Privacy

Daily Question does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed to be safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can delete the app at any time to remove all associated data from your device.
